Where it all started

Wynyard footbal club was originally started by the now chairman Graham Parry in 1999.
Graham and his wife Gill started a fun session to coach primary school children football.
It was held every Saturday morning and Graham and Gills children helped with the coaching.
As other residents joined in to help it wasn't long before the sessions developed into teams,
eventually gaining league status with our first team in 2004.
Wynyard has continued to grow and now has 11 teams ranging from under 7's to a mens team.
Next season will see the launch of our first girls team!
Graham is the first to say that the football in the community is also attributed to the many volunteers who consistently give their time
Graham continues to be heavily involved and can be regulary seen coaching at the Saturday morning club.
